m3 challenge

The mathworks math modeling (m3) challenge was a 14-hour programming challenge focused on modeling the adoption of electric trucks.
Our 3-person group worked to create both mathematical models and visual representations of said models.
I created the MATLAB implementation and visualization of our falling-marginal-cost model for adoption and a python simulation describing how electric trucks would move through a highway.

The source code is not publicly available
