trashnet

Trashnet was created by a 6-person group for the MIT launch competition and lived from late 2017 to spring 2018.
Trashnet was a retrained neural network that used the TensorFlow library to identify and separate garbage from recycling.
Trashnet was able to identify most common forms of recyling but ran into a problems with shredded paper.

It was based on this tutorial.
